  • How will I get to the Farm once I arrive in the UK?


    We have booked a 61 seater coach to transport the yuvashaktis from Chelsham Ashram, London to Blossom farm and back at the end of the seminar.
    Chelsham Ashram departure to the Farm: 16:00 (21st Aug).
    Return from farm to Chelsham Ashram: approx 16:00 (27thAug) and arrive at Chelsham Ashram between 18:15 - 18:25
    On arrival and departure please take into account the time taken to travel to and from Chelsham to your respective airports.

    Address:
    Chelsham Ashram: 44 Chelsham Rd, London SW4 6NP
    Blossom Farm: Warwick CV35 0EL
